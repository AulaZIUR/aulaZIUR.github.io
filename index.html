In the "Aula ZIUR" teachers and students of computer engineering and artificial intelligence collaborate with the company ZIUR with the aim of expanding our knowledge in various fields of cybersecurity. In our meetings we will work on new concepts of pentesting, networking, reversing, security in industrial environments and much more. In addition, anyone familiar with a topic is encouraged to share their knowledge!
